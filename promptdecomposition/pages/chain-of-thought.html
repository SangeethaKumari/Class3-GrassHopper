Chain-of-Thought

Enabling Logical Reasoning

Overview

Chain-of-Thought (CoT) prompting encourages the model to explain its reasoning step-by-step before arriving at a final answer.

The "Let's Think Step-by-Step" Magic

Simply adding the phrase "Let's think step by step" can significantly improve the model's performance on complex math, logic, and multi-step reasoning tasks.

Example

Prompt: If I have 3 oranges and buy 2 more, then give 1 to a friend, how many do I have? Let's think step by step.

Output: 
1. Start with 3 oranges.
2. Buy 2 more: 3 + 2 = 5.
3. Give 1 away: 5 - 1 = 4.
Answer: 4.

When to Use

Crucial for math problems, logical puzzles, and any task where the process is as important as the result.
