Iterative Prompting

The Art of Refinement

Overview

Iterative prompting is the process of talking back to the model, refining its output through multiple turns of conversation.

The Feedback Loop

Instead of expecting perfection in one go, you treat the model as a collaborator. You ask for a draft, provide feedback, and ask for a revision.

Process

  1. Prompt: Generate a 100-word draft about AI.
  2. Feedback: "Make it sound more professional and focus on ethics."
  3. Result: A refined, high-quality response.

When to Use

Always. Prompting is rarely a "one-and-done" task. Iteration is the hallmark of advanced prompt engineering.
