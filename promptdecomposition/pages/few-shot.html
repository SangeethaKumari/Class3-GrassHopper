Few-Shot Prompting

Guiding the Model with Examples

Overview

Few-shot prompting provides the model with a few examples (shots) to demonstrate the desired output format, style, or logic.

Why It's Powerful

By providing examples, you reduce ambiguity. The model learns the "pattern" you want it to follow, which is essential for complex formatting or niche tasks.

Example

Prompt: 
Input: Apple -> Output: Fruit
Input: Carrot -> Output: Vegetable
Input: Laptop -> Output: 

Output: Electronic Device

When to Use

Use few-shot when you need the model to output a very specific format or when zero-shot is failing to capture the nuance of your request.
